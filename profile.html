About me

I'm Danielle Burke {well that's what my parents decided they wanted to call me for the rest of my life üòÄ}. I am a self taught Artist and I currently mostly work with acrylic paint on canvas. Art has changed my life and I'm hoping, at the very least, to bring some positive vibrations into the life of others with my artwork. I created my first "masterpiece" in 2014 as a gift for my {insert loved one here : ) } and ever since then I've managed to lose and find myself over and over again in front of the canvas.
Mainly based on acrylics, my work is the result of a long and ongoing experiment with different techniques and learning through imperfections. My work will undoubtedly keep evolving and continue to evolve on my artistic journey.
